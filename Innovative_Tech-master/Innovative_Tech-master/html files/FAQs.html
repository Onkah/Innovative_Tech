Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How many driving lessons do I need to pass?

The number of lessons needed depends on your experience and personal progress. If you are already able to drive and you are comfortable behind the wheel, 5 to 10 lessons should be enough to brush up on the requirements needed to pass your test A new driver would need about 20 hours of training with practise in-between.


Q: If I passed my learners in one province, can I do my driver’s test in another province?

Yes, you can. You are not restricted to the city or province wherein your learners was issued.


Q: Do I need to pay upfront for my lessons?

Single lessons are paid for on the day of the lesson. Packaged deals are usually paid upfront depending on the arrangement you have with your instructor.


Q: If I pass my driver’s test in an automatic car, will my license allow me to drive a manual vehicle?

No. If you pass in an automatic vehicle, you will only be license for an automatically vehicle.
